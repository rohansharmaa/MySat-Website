About


MySat is built entirely by students under the guidance of experienced teachers and research scholars. It took nearly two and a half years to go from planning to execution to launch. It's main goal is to collect and transmit atmospheric data, enabling a deeper study into weather patterns of the region and to predict future climatic condtions based on current temperature, cloud density, humidity, etc.

People from all over the world, from different backgrounds, have come together as a team to make this possible.
